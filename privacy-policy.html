Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Thank you for using Canolia Note

We prioritize the security of your personal information. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our App.

1. Information Collection

We may collect the following information:

  • Note Data: Notes you write, including titles and formatted text.
  • Preferences: Settings you choose within the App, such as Themes (Aurora, Neumorphic, etc.) and time format (24-hour system).
  • Device Information: Basic data such as OS version and device model used to improve the App's performance.

2. Use of Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the App's features function correctly.
  • To store and organize your notes systematically.
  • To remember your chosen settings and preferences.

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All your notes are primarily stored directly on your device (Phone).
  • Cloud Sync: If you use the Sync feature, your data will be stored on a secure Cloud Server. Please note that this feature is currently in a beta/testing phase.

4. Third-party Services

When you share your notes as PDF, PNG, or Text to other applications (e.g., Viber, Telegram, WhatsApp), the privacy policies of those specific third-party applications will apply.

5. Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You can edit or delete your notes within the App at any time. If you uninstall the App, all data stored in your Local Storage will be permanently removed.

7. Contact Us
